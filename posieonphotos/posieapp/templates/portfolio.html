FREE FALL

Red, yellow, green,
then grey, brown
then repeat.
until you are tired of gazing at
leaves changing colours and
losing their lives falling into
the ground gracefully.

Blue is the sky and golden is the sun
Life stretches
at the end of the thought.
Free fall.
Or fall free.
You decide.

LIVE

Do not wait
for tomorrow
or summer.
Take your day,
and do something!
You are young,
and brave.
Coffee will not get warmer.
Adventure will not wait.
Because:
tomorrow
is
a myth.
Live
today!

LOST SOUL

wandering around to collect

FLAME

At the end of the horizon
where the sky meets the earth,
it sparkles flames
of lust, of passion, of sweetness,
of harmony, of dreams,
of things that aren't real...

I stand still, simply just embracing
the falling of the sky,
collapsing and melting
into the darkness of the night,
of magic, of shining starts,
connecting souls in the vast
and mesmerising galaxy.
